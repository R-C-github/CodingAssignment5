Ingredients

  • 3 cups All-purpose Flour
  • 2 cups Chocolate Chips
  • 1 cup Butter
  • 1 cup White Sugar
  • 1 cup Chopped Walnuts (optional)
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 2 teaspoons Hot water
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â° F.
  2. In a large bowl, beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer until the batter is smooth
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time into the batter
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water
  6. Add baking soda and salt into the batter
  7. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts (optional) into the batter
  8. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets
  9. Bake the cookies in the oven until their edges are browned, about 10 minutes
  10. Let cookies cool on the baking sheet before cooling them on a wire rack.
